Project Name

A new wine bar in Rosendale, NY needed a large millwork piece to separate their dining room from their kitchen as well as store and display their collection of wine and glassware. They also needed a water station built around the existing stainless steel sink in the same room. This design/build project was assembled on a tight budget to fit the space precisely while remaining removable in case the bar ever relocates.

Location: Rosendale, NY
Year: 2025
Status: Completed
